  • Where is Casa Moldovan Located?

    Casa Moldovan is located in the charming spa town of Ocna Șugatag, in the heart of Maramureș, Romania. Known for its saltwater pools, healing mineral baths, and beautiful natural surroundings, Ocna Șugatag is the perfect place to relax while also being close to the region’s famous wooden churches, monasteries, and traditional villages.

  • Nearby Attractions:

    Just steps away from Casa Moldovan, guests can enjoy the charm of Ocna Șugatag’s famous saltwater pools, therapeutic spa treatments, and open-air baths formed from historic salt mines. Lake Gavril offers a peaceful setting for a relaxing stroll, while the Royal Forest Nature Reserve (Pădurea Crăiască) invites you to explore ancient oak trees and unspoiled natural beauty. The village itself is also home to beautifully preserved wooden churches, each carrying centuries of tradition and craftsmanship.

  • Day Trips & Regional Highlights

    A short drive from the property opens the door to some of Maramureș’s most iconic attractions. Visit the stunning Bârsana Monastery, discover winter sports at the Cavnic Ski Resort, or immerse yourself in history at the Memorial of the Victims of Communism in nearby Sighetu Marmației. For something truly unique, the world-famous Merry Cemetery in Săpânța, with its colorful and humorous tombstones, offers a glimpse into the spirit of Maramureș culture.
